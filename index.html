About

At Tidal Bistro, we invite you to embark on a culinary journey inspired by the rhythm of the sea. Nestled in the heart of Portland, Maine, our restaurant is a haven for seafood enthusiasts and lovers of coastal flavors.

Our passion for the ocean extends beyond the kitchen; it's ingrained in every aspect of our restaurant. From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be transported to a maritime oasis, where the ambiance echoes the soothing ebb and flow of the tides.

At Tidal Bistro, we believe in celebrating the freshest catch of the day. Our chefs meticulously select the finest ingredients sourced from local fishermen and sustainable fisheries, ensuring that every dish reflects the essence of the sea.

Whether you're craving succulent oysters on the half shell, decadent lobster bisque, or expertly grilled swordfish, our menu offers a tantalizing array of seafood delights to satisfy every palate. Pair your meal with a handcrafted cocktail or a selection from our curated wine list for the perfect culinary experience.

But Tidal Bistro is more than just a restaurant; it's a gathering place where friends and family come together to share laughter, stories, and unforgettable meals. We pride ourselves on providing exceptional service in a warm and inviting atmosphere, where every guest feels like part of our ocean-inspired family.

Join us at Tidal Bistro and experience the magic of the sea, one delicious bite at a time.

Dive into flavor. Embrace the tide. Welcome to Tidal Bistro.

Awards & Accolades

At Tidal Bistro, we take pride in our commitment to culinary excellence and exceptional dining experiences. Over the years, our dedication to crafting exquisite seafood dishes and providing a welcoming atmosphere has been recognized by esteemed industry organizations and publications. We are honored to have received the following awards:

Culinary Excellence Awards:
  • Winner of the "Best Seafood Restaurant" by Portland Seafood Society
  • Recipient of the "Outstanding Seafood Cuisine" award from Maine Food & Drink Association
  • Voted as a top dining destination for seafood lovers by Portland Food Critics Association
Interior Design Recognition:
  • Awarded for "Best Restaurant Interior Design" by Portland Design Guild
  • Recognized for our coastal-inspired ambiance and innovative decor by Maine Interior Design Magazine
  • Featured in Portland Design Blog for our unique blend of maritime charm and modern aesthetics

These accolades serve as a testament to our unwavering commitment to delivering memorable dining experiences and creating a welcoming environment that delights both the palate and the senses.

Our Team

Sebastian Marlin

With a passion for seafood that runs as deep as the ocean, Chef Sebastian Marlin is renowned for his mastery of coastal cuisine. Born and raised in a fishing village, Sebastian's culinary journey is steeped in tradition and innovation. His specialties include delicately grilled swordfish, marinated in a secret blend of herbs and spices passed down through generations. Sebastian's commitment to sustainability shines in his dishes, as he expertly pairs fresh catch with locally sourced produce, creating harmonious flavors that celebrate the bounty of the sea.

Isabella Saltair

Chef Isabella Saltair brings a touch of elegance to every plate with her refined palate and exquisite culinary creations. Inspired by her coastal upbringing, Isabella's specialties encompass the artistry of seafood, showcasing a delicate balance of flavors and textures. Her signature dish, the lobster bisque, is a symphony of rich, velvety broth infused with the essence of fresh Maine lobster and finished with a hint of brandy. Isabella's commitment to culinary excellence and attention to detail elevate every dish to a culinary masterpiece, leaving diners craving for more.

Santiago Bayshore

A culinary maverick with a flair for innovation, Chef Santiago Bayshore brings a modern twist to classic seafood dishes, pushing the boundaries of flavor and creativity. Hailing from a family of fishermen, Santiago's upbringing instilled in him a deep respect for the sea and its bounty. His specialties include the tantalizing seafood ceviche, bursting with vibrant colors and bold flavors, and his signature grilled octopus, tenderized to perfection and served with a zesty citrus glaze. Santiago's culinary philosophy revolves around honoring tradition while embracing innovation, resulting in dishes that captivate the senses and ignite the imagination.
